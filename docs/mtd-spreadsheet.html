Common questions

The spreadsheet path, answered.

Is this really HMRC-compliant?

Yes. HMRC's own guidance permits spreadsheet plus bridging software as a valid MTD for Income Tax submission path. The pack includes a shortlist of HMRC-recognised bridging tools. GOV.UK source.

Do I need to buy the bridging software separately?

You need one bridging tool. The pack lists free options and one-time-paid options at roughly £15 to £30. You won't be paying a monthly subscription either way.

What if I'm not yet mandated for MTD?

The workbook works for voluntary sign-up too. Same templates, same bridging guidance. Use it to practise before your phase date. The £20k threshold lands April 2028.

What if the rules change?

We update the templates when HMRC moves rates, thresholds or deadlines, and you re-download. Lifetime updates are built into the price.
