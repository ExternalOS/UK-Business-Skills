Questions

Before you buy

  1. Q1What do I need to open it?

    A spreadsheet app. The workbook is a standard .xlsx and opens in Excel, Numbers or Google Sheets. The guide and walkthrough are PDFs. You download the files and they're yours, no login, no subscription.

  2. Q2What happens when the rates change?

    You get the updated file, free, in the same order line as your purchase. The figures live in named cells, so an April update is a re-export, not a rewrite.

  3. Q3Is this tax advice?

    No. It's a tool that does the working using the current HMRC figures, with the sources cited so you can check it. For a decision that turns on your specific circumstances, speak to an accountant.
