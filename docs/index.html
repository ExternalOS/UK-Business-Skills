# IR35 Status Assessment, UK 2025/26

ExternalOS skill file. Last updated 9 Apr 2026. Built against current case law.

## Purpose

Determine whether a contract falls inside or outside IR35 (off-payroll working rules) under HMRC's published guidance and the leading UK case law. Output: a written status decision, a CEST comparison, and a Status Determination Statement (SDS) ready to send.

## Reference framework

  • ITEPA 2003 Part 2 Chapter 8, the IR35 statute
  • HMRC's CEST tool decision logic, current at April 2026
  • Atholl House Productions Ltd v HMRC [2022] EWCA Civ 501
  • HMRC v PGMOL [2024] UKSC 29
  • Kickabout Productions Ltd v HMRC [2022] EWCA Civ 502

## How the skill works

Walk the user through 14 questions covering: mutuality of obligation, right of substitution, control over work, financial risk, integration, exclusivity, intent and pay structure. Score against the three-stage Ready Mixed Concrete test as restated by the Court of Appeal in Atholl House.

## Output formats supported

  • Plain-English status decision (1 paragraph)
  • Structured SDS document (HMRC-format, ready for the client)
  • CEST comparison table (your reasoning vs CEST output)
  • Risk register entry (for accountant or IFA records)
